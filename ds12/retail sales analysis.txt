Retail Sales Dashboard Project
Objectives:-
Analyze retail sales data to uncover business insights
Identify top-performing products, regions, and customers
Track KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
Visualize trends for decision-making
Build an interactive dashboard

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Revenue
Total Orders
Average Order Value (AOV)
Top Selling Product
Revenue by Region
Category-wise Sales
Monthly Sales Trend
Customer Segmentation (New vs Regular vs Premium)
